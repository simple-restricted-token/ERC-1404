ERC 1404

An open standard for security tokens

Download View on Github

Designed with input from top issuers, securities law firms, and exchanges.
The EIP is open for comment here.

Investor Limits

Set maximum number of investors to enforce domestic or international securities laws and comply with registration exemptions.

Investor Whitelist

Know who your investors are at any time and maintain a whitelist of investor addresses. From re-running sanctions checks to requiring accredited investors only, the simple restricted token standard helps token issuers manage their investor lists.

Global Standards

Integrate one standard for global adoption while meeting your compliance requirements. Supports branded standards like Polymath's ST-20 or Harbor's R-Token. Example implementations.

Issuers

If you're seeking to issue a security token, ERC 1404 provides all the tools you need to follow your counsel's guidance with respect to compliance requirements.

Exchanges

This standard was designed with significant input from the major exchanges to be interoperable across exchanges and easy to integrate on any stack. If this standard does not meet your needs, please open up a Github issue here with your request.

Legal Counsel

It may be challenging to navigate transfer restrictions in the open source world of blockchain. ERC 1404 provides the tools necessary for your issuers to comply with domestic and international legal requirements.
